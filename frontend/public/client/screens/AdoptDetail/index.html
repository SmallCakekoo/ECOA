Snake Plant

The Snake Plant is the ultimate survivor. Strong, stoic, and elegant, with tall leaves shaped like green spears brushed with yellow or silver.

More About

The Snake Plant is often called the ultimate survivor among houseplants. Native to West Africa, it has earned a reputation for being almost indestructible. Its tall, upright leaves rise like green spears, sometimes edged or streaked with silver, cream, or yellow, giving it a striking architectural look that fits both modern and traditional interiors.
